## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of `kinetic-core` seriously. If you find a vulnerability, please do **NOT** open a public issue.

### How to Report

Please email the core maintainer directly at **info@kineticmcp.com**.

In your report, please include:
- The version(s) of `kinetic-core` affected.
- A proof of concept or steps to reproduce the issue.
- Any potential impact.

### Response Time

We are committed to addressing security vulnerabilities promptly. You can expect:
- An acknowledgement within 48 hours.
- A status update within 1 week.

Thank you for helping keep the Kinetic ecosystem safe!
